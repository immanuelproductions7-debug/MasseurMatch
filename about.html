About MasseurMatch

Bridging the gap between skilled therapists and those who need healing.

Our Mission

At MasseurMatch, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality massage therapy. We're building the intelligent platform that connects skilled massage therapists with clients who need their expertise. By combining human touch with smart technology, we're making wellness more accessible.

Why We Started

Finding a good massage therapist shouldn't be a guessing game. Therapists shouldn't have to juggle multiple platforms to find clients. We saw a gap in the market and decided to bridge it with a solution that works for everyone.

What Makes Us Different

  • Intelligent Matching: Our AI learns preferences and specialties to make perfect connections.
  • Therapist-First Design: Built with feedback from massage professionals.
  • Client Peace of Mind: Verified therapists, transparent pricing, secure payments.
  • Community Focused: Supporting local wellness practitioners.
